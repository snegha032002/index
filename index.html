Beagle - The Adorable Hound

The Beagle is a breed of small hound, similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound. The beagle is a scent hound, developed primarily for hunting hare. With a great sense of smell and tracking instinct, beagles are employed as detection dogs for prohibited agricultural imports and foodstuffs in quarantine. Their size, good temper, and lack of inherited health problems make them popular pets.
